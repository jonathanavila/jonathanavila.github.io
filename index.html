Jonathan Avila





Hi, I'm Jonathan,


I'm a Ecuadorean-American from Queens who is passionate about applying design thinking and data analytics to improve people's daily lives. I recently graduated with my Bachelor's in Economics from Penn and will be staying in Philadelphia to finish my Master's in Computer Science.

Once I graduate, I plan on helping solve real-world problems such as transportation and home-purchasing by leveraging technology. This summer, I am a Product Management intern @ Redfin, where I lead new business launches, design agent-facing features and ship software for our Listings and Redfin Now businesses.

Currently, I'm also working with Professor Francis DiTraglia to develop a new course on statistical learning and inference. In my free time, I like to read graphic novels and hike tall mountains, as well as train for upcoming the Philadelphia Half Marathon.
