Hi, I'm Jonathan,


I'm a Ecuadorean-American from Queens who is passionate about applying design thinking and data analytics to improve people's daily lives. I recently graduated with my Bachelor's in Economics from Penn and will be staying in Philadelphia to finish my Master's in Computer Science. Currently, I'm also working with Professor Francis DiTraglia to develop a new course on statistical learning and inference. In my free time, I like to read graphic novels and hike tall mountains, as well as train for the Philadelphia Half Marathon.
